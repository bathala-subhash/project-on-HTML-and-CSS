About Us - Mackinlay

Mackinlay is an Indian multinational conglomerate, headquartered in Mumbai. Its businesses include energy, petrochemicals, natural gas, retail, telecommunications, mass media, and textiles. Mackinlay is the largest public company in India by market capitalization and revenue, and the 100th largest company worldwide. It is India's largest private taxpayer and largest exporter, accounting for 7% of India's total merchandise exports.

History

Mackinlay was founded in the early 20th century and has since grown into one of the most influential corporations in India. With a strong presence in various industries, Mackinlay continues to lead with innovation and sustainability.
